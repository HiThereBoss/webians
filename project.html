App Requirements

Who are the target users of the app or apps? Are they more likely to be mobile or desktop users? Do they have any other special characteristics or needs?

The target users for the app are people who would like to purchase cakes or bouquets for special occasions such as birthdays, anniversaries, and weddings. They are most likely to be mobile users preferring a quick and convenient way to order cakes and bouquets. A user would require customization options like personalized messages, cake flavours, sizes and flower arrangements as per the occasion.

Are there any accessibility requirements to accommodate users with disabilities?

There will be accessibility requirements to accommodate users with disabilities since this app is specially designed to assist them as well. Alt text for images will be required.

Will users primarily use the app during specific hours or at specific times of the year?

Users may use the app more often during special occasions and holidays (e.g. Christmas, graduations).

Will the app require regular content updates?

Yes, the app will regularly be updated for new information about cakes and bouquets.

What basic, static information should be available through the app or apps? (For example, company name, address, contact info, etc.)

The company name, contact information, address, list of available baked goods and bouquets, information about products, customization options, pricing details, business hours, and delivery and pickup options must be available.

What are some important design elements of the look and feel of the web app? Is there anything that the client does not want to see in the design of their web app?

Some important design elements include a consistent colour palette that reflects the identity of the client, clear and readable fonts, high-quality visuals, and a layout that is easy to navigate.

What data does the client need to be managed and/or presented to the user? (For example, information on users, products, user-generated content, schedules, statistics, teams, leaderboards, user badges and trophies, etc.)

The data needed to be managed includes information on users (address, contact information, etc), information about their products (cake flavors and sizes, types of flowers, availability), orders and transactions (order placement & tracking, payment processing), and scheduling and delivery.
